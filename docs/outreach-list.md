# AI/Tech newsletter outreach — target list & tracker

_Built June 2026. The B2B wedge from [`market-and-gtm.md`](./market-and-gtm.md): turn a
newsletter into a branded daily podcast._

## The key finding (read this first)

Every **mega AI newsletter (≈1M+ subs) is already taken** — they have their own podcast
and/or are owned by a media company. Don't waste sends on them. Your real targets are
**independent mid-tier newsletters (≈5k–150k subs) with no audio version.**

**Best contact channel (in order):**
1. **Subscribe, then reply to one of their issues.** Highest deliverability + reply rate —
   you're a known contact, not a cold stranger, and your sample link won't hit spam.
2. **X / Twitter DM** the operator (most AI newsletter founders live on X and reply fast).
3. Public partnerships/founder email **only if they list one** (most don't).

> ⚠️ Don't cold-email scraped/guessed addresses — bounces and spam complaints hurt the
> deliverability of the exact sample links you're sending. Reply-to-subscription beats it.

## How to build the list to ~20 (30–60 min)

1. **Beehiiv / Substack → Discover →** filter **Technology / AI**. Sort by mid-tier, not the top.
2. **X search:** `"AI newsletter"`, `"daily AI"`, `beehiiv AI` — find solo operators posting their issues.
3. **Directories:** InboxReads, newsletterinsight, topainewsletters, Readless "best AI newsletters."
4. **Qualify each (the filter):** independent/solo · ≥~5k subs · publishes often · **no podcast/audio**.
5. Add each as a **`prospect` in Admin → Clients**, and fill a row below.

## The message (paste-ready)

**Cold (reply-to / DM):**
> Subject: turned [Newsletter] into a 6-min podcast
>
> Hi [Name] — I read [Newsletter] and turned your latest issue into a two-host AI podcast,
> fully automated: **[sample link]**. If your readers would tap a daily ~6-min audio version
> — zero work for you, new episode every morning, your branding — I'll set up the feed.
> Worth a listen?

**Follow-up (~4 days):**
> Quick nudge — second episode from this week's issue so you can hear it's consistent:
> **[link 2]**. Happy to stand up a branded feed on a free 2-week trial to test with readers.

## Timely angle (use it)

Beehiiv launched **native podcasting (Apr 2026)** — newsletter platforms are racing into audio.
Framing: *"Audio is clearly where newsletters are going — but the DIY tools still mean you record
and edit. Mine is fully done-for-you, daily."* Creates urgency + positions you above DIY.

## Per-prospect sample (in the admin)

Briefings → **New briefing** → title `"[Newsletter] Daily"`, prompt = their topic / latest issue
themes → **Generate episode** → copy the audio link → paste into the message.
